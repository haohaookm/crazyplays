Clumsy Bird

Test your reflexes in this addictive flying adventure

How to Play

Press spacebar or click/tap on the screen to make the bird flap its wings and fly upward. Navigate through the pipes without touching them. Each pipe you successfully pass earns you points. The game ends when you hit a pipe or the ground.

Game Features

  • Simple one-touch gameplay suitable for all ages
  • Colorful graphics with charming pixel art style
  • Progressively challenging obstacles
  • Score tracking to monitor your progress
  • Addictive gameplay that keeps you coming back for "just one more try"

Tips & Strategies

Focus on keeping a steady rhythm rather than making sudden movements. Try to maintain a middle position when possible to give yourself more reaction time. Remember that timing is everything - short, controlled taps work better than long presses. Don't panic when approaching difficult gaps; stay calm and maintain your rhythm.
