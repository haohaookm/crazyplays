Pixel Survivors

Experience nostalgic pixel graphics in this challenging survival adventure

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character through the pixel world. Collect power-ups, defeat enemies, and survive as long as possible. Each level brings more challenging enemies and better rewards!

Game Features

  • Beautiful retro pixel art graphics
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Multiple character upgrades and abilities
  • Nostalgic chiptune soundtrack
  • Simple controls but challenging gameplay

Tips & Strategies

Prioritize collecting experience gems to level up quickly. When choosing upgrades, focus on attack speed early, then damage and area effects. In later waves, defensive upgrades become more important for survival.
